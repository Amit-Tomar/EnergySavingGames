--------------------------
Smart Buyer
--------------------------

This tool will guide player to understand about their appliance consumption and buy accordingly.

-------------
How to use
-------------

• To know about your appliance, just click on the appliance icon
• Selected appliance will be visible with a green colour background
and unselected appliance will be visible with light grey colour.
• Multiple appliance selection is possible.
• To unselect an appliance, click again.
• Total power consumption is shown at the bottom of the page.
